About Our Class

Welcome to the Computer Science and Business Systems (CSBS) class at Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology. We aim to combine the power of computer science with business insights to create future-ready tech leaders.

Our curriculum focuses on the latest trends in technology, including artificial intelligence, machine learning, cloud computing, and business analytics. We encourage a hands-on learning approach with real-world projects and industry collaborations.
